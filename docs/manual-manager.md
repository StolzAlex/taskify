This guide covers everything in the [Employee Guide](manual-employee.md) **plus** the additional capabilities available to managers.

## 1. Customer Accounts

Go to **Customers** in the navigation bar.

### Creating a customer account

1. Fill in the **Name**, **Email**, and **Password** fields in the *Create Customer* form.
2. Optionally assign the customer to a **Group**.
3. Click **Create**.

The customer can then log in at `/customer-login` with the credentials you set.

### Editing a customer

Click the pencil icon on any customer row to change their name, email, password, or group assignment.

### Deactivating / reactivating

Click **Deactivate** to prevent a customer from logging in without deleting their tickets. Click **Activate** to restore access.

---

## 2. Customer Groups

Groups let you organise customers by company or team. They also serve as a filter on the ticket dashboard, so agents can quickly focus on one customer's tickets.

### Creating a group

In the *Groups* panel on the Customers page, enter a group name and click **Create Group**.

### Renaming or deleting

Use the pencil icon to rename. Use the trash icon to delete — the group is removed but its customers are not deleted; they become ungrouped.

### Assigning customers to groups

Set the group when creating a customer, or edit an existing customer and change the *Group* dropdown.

---

## 3. Editing Employees

Managers can edit **staff employees** (employees without admin or manager privileges) in addition to their own profile.

Go to **Admin → Employees** and click the pencil icon on a staff row to change their name, email, or password.

> Managers cannot create new employees, deactivate accounts, or edit other managers and admins. Those actions require admin privileges.
