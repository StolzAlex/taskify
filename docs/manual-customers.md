# Taskify – Customer Guide

## Logging In

Go to the portal URL provided by your support team and click **Customer Login**. Enter your email address and password.

---

## Submitting a Ticket

1. Click **New Ticket** on your dashboard (or fill in the form on the home page if you don't have an account).
2. Enter a **Subject** and describe your issue.
3. Click **Submit**.

You'll receive a confirmation email with a link to track your ticket.

---

## Tracking Your Ticket

Log in and click the ticket subject to open it. You can also use the tracking link from your confirmation email — no login required.

---

## Replying

Scroll to the bottom of the ticket, type your message, and click **Send Reply**. Your support team is notified automatically.

---

## Ticket Statuses

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| Open | Received, not yet started |
| In Progress | Being worked on |
| Resolved | Fixed — reply if you need more help |
| Closed | No further action expected |

---

## Need Help With Your Account?

Contact your support team — they can reset your password or reactivate your account.
