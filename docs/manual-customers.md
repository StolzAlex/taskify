## 1. Accessing the Portal

Your support team will provide you with the portal URL.

### Logging in

1. Go to the portal URL and click **Customer Login**.
2. Enter your **e-mail address** and **password**.
3. Click **Login**.

Your account is created by your support contact. You will receive a welcome e-mail with your credentials when the account is set up.

### Language

Click the language button in the top navigation bar to switch between **English** and **German**.

---

## 2. Submitting a Ticket

You can submit a support request in two ways:

### While logged in

1. Click **New Ticket** on your dashboard.
2. Fill in the **Subject** and describe your issue in the text field.
3. Click **Submit**.

You will immediately see a confirmation message with a link to track your ticket.

### Without an account (public form)

If the public submission form is enabled, you can submit a ticket at the portal home page without logging in.

1. Enter your **e-mail address**, **subject**, and description.
2. Click **Submit**.

A confirmation e-mail is sent to the address you provided, containing a link to track your ticket.

> **Tip:** Submitting with an account gives you a full dashboard to manage all your tickets in one place.

## 3. My Tickets Dashboard

After logging in you see your personal ticket overview.

### Stat cards

Four summary cards are shown at the top:

| Card | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| Open | Tickets received, not yet being worked on |
| In Progress | Tickets actively being handled by support |
| Awaiting reply | Tickets where support has responded and is waiting for you |
| Resolved / Closed | Completed tickets |

Click a card to jump to the corresponding view.

### Views (tabs)

| Tab | Shows |
|-----|-------|
| **All** | All your tickets |
| **Awaiting reply** | Tickets where support has responded — your action is needed |
| **Resolved / Closed** | Completed tickets |

### Searching and filtering

- Use the **search box** to filter tickets by subject or keywords.
- Use the **status dropdown** to show only tickets with a specific status.
- Click **Clear** to remove all filters.

### Reply pending badge

A yellow *Reply from support* badge next to a ticket means support has responded and is waiting for your reply.

## 4. Viewing and Replying to a Ticket

### Opening a ticket

Click the ticket subject to open the full detail view.

> Resolved and closed tickets are shown as plain text (not clickable) in the *All* tab. Switch to the *Resolved / Closed* tab to access them, or use the tracking link from your confirmation e-mail.

### Reading messages

Messages from the support team are shown below the original ticket description.
Each message shows the date and time it was sent.

### Replying

1. Scroll to the bottom of the ticket page.
2. Type your reply in the text editor.
3. Optionally attach a file using the file upload field.
4. Click **Send Reply**.

Support will be notified of your reply automatically.

### Tracking without an account

If you submitted a ticket without a customer account, use the tracking link from your confirmation e-mail. It looks like:

```
/status/<your-token>
```

This link lets you view the ticket status and messages without logging in. Keep this link safe — anyone with it can view your ticket.

## 5. Ticket Statuses

| Status | What it means |
|--------|--------------|
| **Open** | Your ticket has been received and is waiting to be picked up |
| **In Progress** | A support agent is actively working on your request |
| **Resolved** | Support has resolved your issue — please confirm or reply if you need further help |
| **Closed** | The ticket is fully closed. No further action is expected |

You receive an e-mail whenever the status of your ticket changes.

---

## 6. Email Notifications

You automatically receive e-mails in the following situations:

| Event | E-mail sent |
|-------|-------------|
| Ticket submitted | Confirmation with tracking link |
| Support sends you a message | Notification with a link to the ticket |
| Ticket status changes | Update with the new status |
| Ticket resolved or closed | Final notification |

All e-mails are sent in the **language you had selected** when you submitted the ticket.

E-mails come from your support team's address. Please add it to your contacts to avoid notifications landing in spam.

## 7. Account Issues

### Forgotten password

Contact your support team to have your password reset. They can update it in the customer management panel and provide you with a new one.

### Account deactivated

If you cannot log in, your account may have been deactivated. Contact your support team for assistance.

### Changing your own password

At this time, password changes must be performed by your support contact. Please reach out to them directly.
